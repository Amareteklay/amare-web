# Adaptation & AI

What does it mean to adapt in the age of artificial intelligence?

We are at an inflection point. The tools we build are beginning to reshape the cognitive landscape as profoundly as writing or the printing press did.

Adaptation is not merely survival — it is the active remaking of the self in relation to a changing world.
